Motivation and Point of Difference

We are very interested in Mealr as several of us have previously, or currently still do subscribe to meal kit services however, these meal kits are somewhat limited when it comes to dietary requirements for the recipes that are produced each week. The most popular meal kit services such as Hello Fresh, Dinnerly, and Marley Spoon do offer meals and ingredients that are vegetarian and contain no added gluten however there are not as many of these meals compared to the meals offered that contain meat or gluten (Marley Spoon, 2021). Further to this, many people do also have requirements of consuming only halal or kosher certified foods. The ingredients provided in these meal kits often don’t state whether they are halal or kosher certified and may cause apprehension for people with this requirement. Meal kit services are also not as readily available for people who do adhere to halal and kosher requirements. The only halal or kosher meal kits available in Melbourne are Halal Dinnerbox and Kosher Meals on Wheels, both of which only provide their customers with pre-packaged and precooked food, limiting the customer on being able to enjoy freshly cooked meals. If a user lives far away from a large city, then the likelihood of there being such service that can deliver to them is very slim.

The point of difference for Mealr is to allow users with very specific dietary requirements to enjoy the convenience of meal planning and not having to think about if the food they are making is okay to consume, while also enjoying their meals being as fresh as possible since they are not pre-packaged. Mealr also allows users to save the cost of delivery if they wish to go to their supermarket or local food store to purchase their ingredients. The price of delivery for meal kits such as Dinnerly, Marley Spoon, and Hello Fresh is $10.00 per box sent (Marley Spoon, 2021). If a user received a box each week, this is an additional $520.00 over the course of a year, which can easily be saved if the user wishes.
